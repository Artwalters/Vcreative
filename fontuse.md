## PS Times

PS Times, a contemporary twist on Times New Roman, has been introduced by Parker, in collaboration with type designer Jesse Amborn. It features modern adjustments like shorter descenders and a variety of ligatures, tailored for digital applications and larger displays.

Inspired by 'lost variations' from the phototype era, PS Times aims for a uniform, tailored look with condensed geometry, improved optical weight, and extensive contextual alternates for versatility. It is designed for bold headlines and larger body copy, with future versions planned to include additional styles like Text, Italic, Display and Antique.
